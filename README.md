# RecallOS

RecallOS is a visual memory assistant for people whose camera roll has become their real inbox.

You screenshot ideas, receipts, products, flights, payment details, conversations, todos, and random "I will need this later" moments all day. RecallOS turns that mess into something you can actually use again.

## The Vibe

Most productivity apps ask you to be disciplined.

RecallOS assumes you are already moving fast, already context-switching, and already capturing everything as screenshots. It meets you there.

Think of it as a second brain for screenshots: save first, sort later, find it when it matters.

## What It Does

- Captures screenshots into a clean personal memory feed
- Reads text, links, dates, QR codes, payment details, and useful entities from images
- Suggests smart actions based on what is inside a screenshot
- Turns screenshots into tasks, reminders, and events
- Organizes related screenshots into stacks
- Lets you share stacks with other people through web links
- Keeps the experience fast, dark, focused, and low-friction

## Why It Exists

Your screenshots are not clutter. They are unfinished decisions.

A link you meant to open.  
A product you wanted to compare.  
A flight detail you did not want to lose.  
A bill, offer, invite, address, quote, idea, or conversation you knew would matter later.

RecallOS gives those loose fragments a place to land.

## Built For

- People who run their life from screenshots
- Founders, students, operators, creators, and high-context workers
- Anyone who saves first and organizes later
- Anyone tired of digging through 4,000 images to find one detail from three weeks ago
